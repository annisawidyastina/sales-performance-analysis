# 📊 Sales Performance Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing sales data to understand business performance, identify trends, and generate actionable insights. The analysis aims to support data-driven decision-making by exploring key metrics such as revenue, profit, and customer behavior.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze total revenue and profit
- Identify top-performing products and categories
- Understand customer demographics
- Explore sales trends over time
- Provide actionable business insights

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- SQL (PostgreSQL)
- CSV Dataset
- Data Visualization (Grafana)

---

## 📂 Dataset Description
The dataset contains sales transaction data with the following key columns:

- Date, Day, Month, Year  
- Customer_Age, Age_Group, Customer_Gender  
- Country, State  
- Product_Category, Sub_Category, Product  
- Order_Quantity, Unit_Cost, Unit_Price  
- Revenue, Cost, Profit  

---

## 🔍 Analysis Performed

### 1. Sales Performance
- Total Revenue
- Total Profit
- Total Quantity Sold

### 2. Time-Based Analysis
- Revenue by Year
- Profit by Month
- Sales Trends

### 3. Product Analysis
- Top Selling Products
- Most Profitable Products
- Performance by Category

### 4. Customer Analysis
- Revenue by Gender
- Revenue by Age Group

### 5. Geographic Analysis
- Revenue by Country
- Revenue by State

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- A small number of products contribute to the majority of revenue
- Certain months show higher sales, indicating seasonal trends
- Specific customer segments generate more revenue
- Some products have high sales but low profit margins

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Focus on high-profit products for marketing strategies
- Evaluate pricing strategy for low-margin products
- Target high-value customer segments
- Optimize sales strategy in top-performing regions

---

## 🚀 Conclusion
This project demonstrates how SQL can be used to analyze business data and generate meaningful insights that support strategic decisions.
